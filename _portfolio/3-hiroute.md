**arXiv preprint (coming soon) · First author.** HiRoute builds a heterogeneous VLM→DiT routing interface on a pretrained Wan video DiT: each DiT block adaptively selects the most suitable layer from a frozen VLM (Qwen) via cross-attention, unifying text-, image-, and video-conditioned generation and editing in a single model.
