**ECCV 2026 · Co-first author.** PSDF generalizes the static SDF field into time-varying parametric curves for end-to-end 4D dynamic surface reconstruction via differentiable iso-surfacing and physically-based rendering, reducing geometry error by over 40% and disentangling geometry, material, and lighting.
