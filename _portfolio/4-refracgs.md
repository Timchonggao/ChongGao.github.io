**ECCV 2026 · Co-author.** RefracGS jointly reconstructs a refractive water surface (a neural height field) and the underwater scene (a 3D Gaussian field) via refraction-aware Gaussian ray tracing based on Snell's law, enabling high-fidelity, view-consistent novel view synthesis.
