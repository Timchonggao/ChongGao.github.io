I am Chong Gao (高冲), an M.S. student in Computer Science at Sun Yat-sen University (advised by Prof. [Guanbin Li](https://guanbinli.com/)). I have been a research intern in the Visual Computing and Learning Lab at Peking University (advised by Prof. [Wenzheng Chen](https://wenzhengchen.github.io/), Prof. Baoquan Chen's group) and at Alibaba. My research centers on 3D/4D computer vision, neural rendering and inverse rendering, and multimodal video generation.

# Research Interests
- 3D/4D reconstruction and inverse rendering (SDF, Mesh, 3D Gaussian Splatting)
- Neural rendering and physically-based rendering (PBR)
- Multimodal video generation and video world models
