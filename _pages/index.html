Chong Gao

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science at Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Guanbin Li. I have interned at the Visual Computing and Learning Lab, Peking University (with Prof. Wenzheng Chen) and at Alibaba. My research focuses on 3D/4D computer vision, neural rendering and inverse rendering, and multimodal video generation.
