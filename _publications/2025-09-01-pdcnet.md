PDC-Net segments pelvic radiation injury (PRI) from MRI by "dividing" local and global patterns in the encoder (multi-direction aggregation, memory-guided context) and "conquering" them with an adaptive fusion decoder, reaching SOTA on the first large-scale PRI dataset.
