**Co-first author.** GeoSplatting guides 3D Gaussian Splatting with an explicit geometry (FlexiCubes surface) and a differentiable PBR renderer with BVH-accelerated ray tracing, achieving SOTA inverse rendering and geometry accuracy (PSNR +3-5 dB, training reduced to ~14 minutes / ~8× faster).
