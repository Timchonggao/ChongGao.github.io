**First author.** HiRoute builds a heterogeneous VLM-to-DiT routing interface on a pretrained Wan video DiT: each DiT block adaptively selects the most suitable layer from a frozen VLM (Qwen) via cross-attention, exploiting the full depth of VLM representations without architecture alignment or VLM training. A single model unifies text-, image-, and video-conditioned generation and editing (T2V / TI2V / TIV2V).
