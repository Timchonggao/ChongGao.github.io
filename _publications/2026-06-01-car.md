CaR (Compression and Retrieval) maintains long-term memory consistency for video world models under complex / revisiting camera trajectories via an attention-driven implicit retrieval mechanism (dual-branch attention + context compression, ~97% fewer tokens), and introduces the UE5-based SceneFly dataset.
