**Co-first author.** PSDF generalizes the static SDF field into time-varying parametric curves, enabling end-to-end 4D dynamic surface reconstruction via differentiable iso-surfacing and physically-based rendering. It achieves SOTA geometry accuracy (geometry error reduced by over 40%, PSNR +~1.5 dB) and disentangles geometry, material, and lighting for relighting and editing.
